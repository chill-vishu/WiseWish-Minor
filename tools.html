How to Use These Tools

Academic Tools

1. CGPA Calculator

Purpose: To calculate your cumulative grade point average (CGPA) based on your semester-wise grades.

How to Use:

  1. Enter the grades and credit hours for each subject.
  2. Add all the subjects for your current semester.
  3. Click "Calculate" to get your CGPA.
  4. The tool will compute the weighted average of your grades across semesters.
2. Percentage Calculator

Purpose: To convert your GPA or CGPA into a percentage format.

How to Use:

  1. Enter your GPA or CGPA in the input field.
  2. Click "Convert to Percentage".
  3. The tool will display the equivalent percentage based on the university conversion formula.
3. SGPA to CGPA Calculator

Purpose: To convert your semester grade point average (SGPA) into CGPA.

How to Use:

  1. Enter your SGPA for each semester.
  2. Add as many semesters as needed.
  3. Click "Calculate CGPA" to see your cumulative score.
  4. The tool will average the SGPA scores to generate the CGPA.
4. Attendance Tracker

Purpose: To keep track of your attendance percentage and ensure you meet attendance requirements.

How to Use:

  1. Enter the total number of classes conducted.
  2. Enter the number of classes you attended.
  3. Click "Calculate Attendance".
  4. The tool will display your current attendance percentage.

Productivity Tools

1. Pomodoro Timer

Purpose: To help you manage study or work time effectively using the Pomodoro technique.

How to Use:

  1. Set a work session of 25 minutes (default Pomodoro).
  2. Click "Start Timer" and work without distractions.
  3. Once the timer ends, take a 5-minute break.
  4. Repeat this process until your work session is complete.
2. Daily Task Manager

Purpose: To plan and track your daily tasks effectively.

How to Use:

  1. Add your tasks for the day by entering them in the task input field.
  2. Mark tasks as completed as you finish them.
  3. Review your progress at the end of the day.
3. Focus Time Calculator

Purpose: To help calculate how much focused work you can achieve in a given day or week.

How to Use:

  1. Input the total number of hours available for focused work.
  2. Enter the duration of your work intervals and breaks.
  3. Click "Calculate Focus Time" to see the total amount of focused time you can work in a day.

Health & Wellness Tools

1. Calorie Intake Calculator

Purpose: To help you calculate your daily calorie needs based on activity level and weight goals.

How to Use:

  1. Enter your age, gender, height, and weight.
  2. Choose your activity level (sedentary, active, etc.).
  3. Click "Calculate" to get your daily calorie intake recommendation.
2. Sleep Cycle Calculator

Purpose: To help you determine the best time to go to bed or wake up to complete a full sleep cycle.

How to Use:

  1. Enter the time you need to wake up or go to bed.
  2. Click "Calculate" to see the optimal times for starting or ending your sleep cycle.
3. Step Counter Estimate

Purpose: To estimate the number of steps you should take daily based on your fitness goals.

How to Use:

  1. Enter your daily activity level and fitness goal.
  2. Click "Calculate Steps" to get an estimate of how many steps you need to take each day.
