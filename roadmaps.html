Welcome to WiseWish Roadmaps

Here you'll find detailed and structured guides to help you navigate through your academic journey. Whether you’re a student of science, mathematics, computer science, or commerce, these roadmaps are designed to give you a step-by-step plan to master each subject. Let’s dive in!

Science Roadmap

1.Foundation: Start with understanding fundamental scientific principles such as biology, chemistry, and physics.
2. Advanced Concepts: Once you have a foundation, explore topics like genetics, organic chemistry, and thermodynamics.
3. Research & Experimentation: Get hands-on with labs, experiments, and fieldwork. This step is essential for understanding how theories work in practice.
4. Specialization: Choose an area of science like environmental science, medical science, or physics and dive deeper into advanced subjects.
5. Practical Applications: Apply scientific knowledge in real-world scenarios through internships or research projects.

Math Roadmap

1. Basic Arithmetic: Begin with mastering arithmetic operations, fractions, and percentages.
2. Algebra & Geometry: Build upon the basics by learning algebra, geometry, and trigonometry.
3. Advanced Mathematics: Study calculus, probability, and statistics to prepare for higher-level mathematical concepts.
4. Mathematical Modeling: Learn how to model real-world problems with mathematics using differential equations and linear algebra.
5. Specialization: Delve into specialized areas like applied mathematics, cryptography, or pure mathematics.

Computer Science Roadmap

1. Programming Basics: Start with learning basic programming languages like Python, C++, or Java.
2. Data Structures & Algorithms: Understand how to optimize code and solve complex problems efficiently.
3. Web Development & Databases: Learn HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and SQL to build interactive websites and manage data.
4. Software Development: Work on full-stack development, mobile app development, or system programming.
5. Advanced Topics: Study artificial intelligence, machine learning, or blockchain technology for cutting-edge development.

Commerce Roadmap

1. Basic Accounting: Begin with fundamentals like balance sheets, profit & loss statements, and financial accounting.
2. Micro & Macro Economics: Understand the basics of how economies work at both small and large scales.
3. Business Law & Ethics: Learn the legal and ethical considerations of running a business.
4. Specialization: Focus on areas such as marketing, finance, or human resources management.
5. Practical Applications: Apply theoretical knowledge in internships, case studies, and projects in the business world.

Study Tips and Tricks

  • Set small, achievable goals for each study session.
  • Take regular breaks to maintain focus and productivity.
  • Use the Pomodoro technique for time management (25 minutes study, 5 minutes break).
  • Teach what you’ve learned to others to reinforce your knowledge.
  • Utilize active recall by summarizing and testing yourself after each study session.
