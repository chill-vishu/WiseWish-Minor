What is Mathematics?

Mathematics is the abstract study of numbers, quantities, shapes, and patterns. It involves the exploration of concepts such as addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division, as well as more complex topics like algebra, geometry, calculus, and statistics. Mathematics provides the tools for solving problems, analyzing data, and understanding relationships between different elements. It is fundamental in various fields, including science, engineering, economics, and everyday life, helping us model real-world situations and make informed decisions. Through logical reasoning and critical thinking, mathematics fosters a deeper understanding of the world around us.
