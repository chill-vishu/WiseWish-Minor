What is Commerce?

Commerce refers to the activity of buying and selling goods and services, encompassing all the processes involved in trading. It includes various aspects like marketing, transportation, finance, and regulation. Commerce can take place on a small scale, like local businesses, or on a larger scale, such as international trade. The goal of commerce is to facilitate the exchange of value between parties, which can ultimately drive economic growth and development.
