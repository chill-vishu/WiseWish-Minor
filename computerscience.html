What is Computer Science?

Computer Science is the study of computers and computational systems, encompassing both the theoretical and practical aspects of information processing. It involves understanding algorithms, programming, data structures, and the principles of software design. Computer scientists develop solutions to complex problems, create software applications, and design computer systems that are efficient and effective. This dynamic field also explores topics like artificial intelligence, cybersecurity, and data analysis, making it essential in today’s technology-driven world. Whether through coding, system architecture, or research, computer science plays a vital role in shaping how we interact with technology and solve real-world challenges.
